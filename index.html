A Massachusetts mother is furious after her 7-year-old daughter’s head was shaved at school for “hygienic reasons,” the New York Daily News reported. Denise Robinson dropped her daughter, Tru, off at Little Heroes Group Home in Dracut with hair that reached past her shoulders. “Her hair was in two pony tails on the side on Saturday and it was braided in the pony tails and there was nothing wrong,” Robinson said. But when she picked Tru up from the group home that afternoon, her hair had been shaved off.
